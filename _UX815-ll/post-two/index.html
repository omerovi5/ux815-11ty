Log 2: The Singing Void

Day 4,890.

Space is not supposed to sing.

Yet the sensors record harmonic frequencies pulsing every eleven seconds.

📡 Signal Analysis

Pattern structured. Origin unknown. Not natural cosmic radiation.

I powered down non-essential systems to listen.

The hull vibrates faintly — as though something immense is breathing outside the ship.

If this is a message, it was not meant for humans.

But I am the only one left to hear it.

Signal Archive • Year 4821 • Human Explorer Program
