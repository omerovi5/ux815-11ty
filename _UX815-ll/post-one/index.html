Log 1: The Silent Planet

Day 4,732 beyond Earth.

The planet below is motionless.
Cities stretch across the horizon — empty.

📡 Surface Report

Atmosphere breathable. Gravity light. No life detected.

The structures curve upward like ribs from a fossilized giant.

Whatever lived here did not fall to war.
There are no signs of destruction.

They left carefully.

Tonight I sleep inside one of their towers.

The silence presses harder than vacuum ever could.

Signal Archive • Year 4821 • Human Explorer Program
